About Us

Lazybear.bn is a home-based business in Kg. Tanah Jambu, selling sweet delicacies such as brownies, cookies and other sweet treats. They specialize in making customized cakes where customers are encouraged to order 7-10 days in advance.

After two years operating under its former name "Valentini Kitchenette", the business re-established itself in 2018 and came back with improved recipes.

Lazybear.bn was started from the demands of baked goods by the founder's family and friends. Gaining confidence after joining bake sales of various events, the business ventured out to selling on online platforms (GoMamam and HeyDomo) and it kept growing until this day.

Since its establishment, the business has received a lot of positive feedbacks and recommendations from its customers and Lazybear.bn has become known as a brand for its cakes and cookies.

Our aim is to ensure that our customers' satisfactions are fulfilled and that they will enjoy our handmade pieces. We also hope to satisfy not only children but also adults.
